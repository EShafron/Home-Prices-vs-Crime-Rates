Data Science & Visualization Bootcamp Project 1: 'There Goes the Neighborhood'

Zillow Housing Data & API Crime Rates

In this first project, we investigated the relationship between reported crime rates & housing values.

Our idea was to identify zip codes & counties where crime was most weighing down property values.
Then, in a subsequent analysis (to be completed), we could identify which of these geographic regions was likely to experience a drop in crime.
These zip codes & counties would be prime choices for oppurtunistic real-estate investment.

In the absence of this data, we compared the average crime rate in counties to the crime rate in specific zip codes in that county.
The idea was this could stand in as zip codes where crime rates were poised to drop (& housing prices were poised to climb).
Based on a simple linear regression, we calculated how much more a median property would be worth if crime in the zip code returned to the county average.
Depending on whether we were looking at median $ per sqft, or median home value index, different crime variables were significant, and different zip codes were returned.
